The Neighbourhood - Sweater Weather


The Neighbourhood is an American band formed in 2011. Its members include Jesse Rutherford, Jeremy Freedman, Zach Abels, Mikey Margott, and Brandon Fried. More information can be found here.
Formed in Los Angeles in 2011, the band centers around lead singer Jesse James Rutherford, guitarists Jeremy Freedman and Zach Abels, bassist Michael Margott, and drummer Brandon Fried. In 2012, the Neighbourhood released the EP I'm Sorry..., featuring such singles as "Sweater Weather" and "Female Robbery."
